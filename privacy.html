RJZ Studio

Privacy Policy · Last updated March 2026

Who We Are

RJZ Studio is an indoor cycling fitness studio. This policy covers how we collect and use information provided by riders during class check-in and participation.

Information We Collect

When you check in for a class, we collect your name, phone number, and optionally your email address. During class, we collect performance data including heart rate, power output (watts), cadence (RPM), calories, and distance.

How We Use Your Information

Your phone number and email are used solely to send you post-ride performance summaries and, in limited cases, operational notifications such as sensor status alerts to studio staff. Your ride data is used to display live stats during class, generate post-ride recaps, and maintain your personal lifetime performance history within the studio.

SMS Messaging

By providing your phone number at check-in, you consent to receive SMS messages from RJZ Studio including post-ride performance summaries. Message frequency varies based on class participation. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for help.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Ride data may be shared with third-party fitness platforms such as Strava only with your explicit authorization via OAuth.

Data Retention

Your information is retained for as long as you are an active rider. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
